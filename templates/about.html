Help! I'm a terminal hipster!

cat file-name | curl -H "X-Respondwith: link" -X POST -d "text=$(</dev/stdin)" {{host_url}}
Notice: The "X-Respondwith: link" is important. Otherwise you will get a redirect response.

You could even alias the curl part of that command:

alias quickpaste="curl -H \"X-Respondwith: link\" -X POST -d \"text=\$(</dev/stdin)\" {{host_url}}"

To make it as simple as:

cat file-name | quickpaste
